# Privacy Policy for eddy

**Last updated: June 17, 2026**

eddy ("the app") is developed by Jithu J. Nair, operating under the developer name DevKami ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use eddy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a) Information you provide
- **Journal entries** — text, photos, and voice recordings you create within the app. This content is stored locally on your device and optionally backed up to your personal Google Drive account.
- **Habit data** — habit names and completion history you create in the Momentum feature.

### b) Information collected automatically
- **Google account email address** — collected only if you choose to sign in with Google for Drive backup. We do not store your email on our servers.
- **Crash logs** — collected automatically via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs. May include device model, OS version, and app state at the time of a crash.
- **App interactions and device identifiers** — collected via Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app (e.g., which features are used). This data is pseudonymous and not linked to your identity.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and improve app functionality
- To back up your data to your personal Google Drive (only if you opt in)
- To diagnose and fix crashes and bugs
- To understand general usage patterns and improve the app

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Local data** (journal entries, habits) is stored on your device using Hive, a local database. It is not transmitted to our servers.
- **Google Drive backup** is stored in your personal Google Drive app data folder, accessible only by you and the app. We use Google's OAuth 2.0 for authentication. We do not have access to your Drive files.
- **All data transmitted** between the app and external services (Google Drive, Firebase) is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except:
- **Firebase (Google)** — for crash reporting and anonymous analytics
- **Google Drive** — for backup, only if you opt in

Both services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

- **Google Sign-In is optional.** All core features work without signing in.
- **You can revoke Google access** at any time via your Google account settings at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
- **You can delete your data** by uninstalling the app (removes local data) and deleting your Drive backup via your Google Drive settings.
- **To request account and data deletion**, contact us at: jithunair95@gmail.com with the subject "Eddy Account Deletion Request"

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

eddy is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
